// MISSION BRIEFING

WHY B.O.L.D EXISTS

Every day, kids all over the world get bullied. They feel alone, scared, and like nobody is coming to help them. Teachers don't always see it. Parents don't always know. But other kids do.

B.O.L.D agents are kids who go undercover in their own schools and neighborhoods. They watch. They report. They protect. They stop bullying before it gets worse.

This is not a club. This is not a game. This is a real operation.

> MISSION: Identify, report, and stop bullying in schools and communities worldwide. Protect every kid who cannot protect themselves. Leave no one behind.
